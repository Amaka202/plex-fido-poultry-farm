Mission

At FIDO POULTRY, Our mission is to be simply better in all we do, and to always deliver responsibly raised poultry with no added hormones or steroids

About this Web Application

The Farmers have a big issue with structure and to achieve that they need a system in place that helps them manage their farm effectively. The app would achieve the following:


        
Dashboard – displays count of existing birds, mortality rate, feeds, drugs, Sales, expenditure, profit and loss.

        
Daily Record system – allows user input the number of birds, feed consumption, water consumption, vaccinations, Drugs taken and mortality. 

      
 Accounting –  Allows user input sales and expenditure showing profits and loss.

        
Disease check - here staff can specify symptoms in a sick chicken and get recommendation on drugs to be administered.

        
User system - that will allow the farm owners and their staff log into the app and carryout certain functions.

            
                
            

        
        
With an application like this farmers are sure to have a full detail of their daily activities on record and can make better decisions from the report. This will enable the farm owner to monitor their workers activities in the farm.
